Our Journey

From vision to Tanzania's #1 real estate platform

2020 - The Vision

CEO Irene Mgimba and Director George William identified the need for a digital real estate platform in Tanzania's growing market.

2021 - Development Begins

Partnered with I&G Technology to develop the platform architecture and initial prototype.

2022 - Beta Launch

Launched beta version with 100 properties and 20 agents in Dar es Salaam.

2023 - Official Launch

Officially launched HouseLink Tanzania with 1,000+ properties and 50+ certified agents across major cities.

2024 - Market Leadership

Became Tanzania's #1 real estate platform with 10,000+ properties, 500+ agents, and expansion to 25+ cities.
